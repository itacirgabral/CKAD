> Criar um Pod com livenessProbe (ex: command: ["cat", "/tmp/healthy"]), e depois deletar o arquivo para ver restart.
```yaml
apiVersion: v1
kind: Pod
metadata:
  labels:
    run: livess
  name: livess
spec:
  containers:
  - image: nginx
    name: livess
    livenessProbe:
      exec:
        command:
        - cat
        - /tmp/healthy
      initialDelaySeconds: 15
      periodSeconds: 10
  restartPolicy: Always
```
